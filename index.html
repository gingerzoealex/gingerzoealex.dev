What Am I Talking About?

Hi, I'm Zoe

During the day I write code, & by night I write code & crochet things.

I love open source & open data, a love inspired by my first developer role with Flax & Teal Ltd in Belfast. I mainly worked with PHP, Laravel, Python and Vue.js, & also used open data.

I moved to Glasgow in 2019 and now I work at the BBC in the Archive Services team, mainly using React, Node.js, & AWS tech like Lambda, S3, CloudFormation.

Things change so quickly - it's more important to have passion for what you do, rather than knowing exactly what you're doing.

I've organised events & volunteered for various organisations, such as Code Co-Op, Farset Labs, & Belfast Tech Families.
